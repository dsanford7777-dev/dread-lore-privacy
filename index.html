Privacy Policy

Dread Lore — @dreadloreofficial

Last updated: May 11, 2026

1. Overview

Dread Lore is an automated horror storytelling channel that publishes AI-generated illustrated videos to TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube. This privacy policy explains how we handle information in connection with our app and content.

2. Data We Collect

Dread Lore does not collect, store, or sell personal information from viewers or followers. We do not operate a website with user accounts, forms, or tracking.

3. Platform APIs

Dread Lore uses the Meta Graph API, TikTok API, and YouTube Data API solely to publish content to our own accounts. We access only the permissions required to post videos and manage our own pages. We do not access, store, or process any data belonging to other users.

4. Third-Party Services

Content is generated using third-party AI services (Anthropic, Fal.ai, ElevenLabs). These services are governed by their own privacy policies. No viewer data is shared with these services.

5. Cookies

This privacy policy page does not use cookies or any tracking technology.

6. Children's Privacy

Dread Lore publishes horror content intended for mature audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.
